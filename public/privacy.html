Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 11, 2026

DownRange ("the app", "we", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

DownRange is designed to work without collecting personal information. Here is what the app accesses:

  • Location data (GPS) — Used only when you tap the GPS or Auto buttons to retrieve your altitude, temperature, and wind data. Location is sent to the Open-Meteo weather API (open-meteo.com) to retrieve current conditions. We do not store, log, or transmit your location to our servers.
  • Local storage — Your gun profiles, DOPE book entries, favorite bullets, and calculator settings are stored locally on your device using browser localStorage. This data never leaves your device.
  • No account required — DownRange does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, or any identifying information
  • We do not track your usage or behavior
  • We do not use analytics or advertising SDKs
  • We do not sell data to third parties
  • We do not use cookies for tracking
  • We do not transmit your DOPE book, profiles, or calculator data to any server

3. Third-Party Services

DownRange uses the following third-party services:

  • Open-Meteo API (open-meteo.com) — A free, open-source weather API used when you request live weather data. Your approximate location coordinates are sent to this service to retrieve temperature, wind speed, and wind direction. Open-Meteo's privacy policy is available at open-meteo.com. No personal data beyond location coordinates is shared.
  • Google Fonts — Font files are loaded from Google's CDN for display purposes. Google's privacy policy applies to this request.

4. Location Permission

The app requests location permission only when you explicitly tap a GPS or Auto button. Location access is used solely to retrieve weather and elevation data for ballistics calculations. The app does not track your location in the background or store GPS coordinates.

5. Data Storage and Security

All user data — gun profiles, DOPE book entries, favorites, and settings — is stored locally on your device using the device's standard local storage mechanism. This data is not transmitted to any server. You can delete all stored data by clearing the app's data through your device settings.

6. Children's Privacy

DownRange is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any data from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Changes will be posted at this URL with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Contact us at hello@godownrange.com

9. Disclaimer

DownRange is a reference tool only. Ballistic calculations are estimates based on physics models and published bullet data. Always verify trajectory data with live range testing. The app developers are not responsible for outcomes resulting from reliance on calculated ballistic data.
